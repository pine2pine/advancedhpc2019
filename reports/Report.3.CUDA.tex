\section{Initial CUDA run}

For the initial run, I used the initial block size of 64 just to compare the performance to that of the OpenMP solution used in labwork #1.

Here's the output of labwork #3 on ICT4 server:

\begin{verbatim}

USTH ICT Master 2018, Advanced Programming for HPC.
Warming up...

\end{verbatim}

And here's the output of labwork #1 (using OpenMP) on the same server:
\begin{verbatim}
student1@ictserver4:/storage/student1/advancedhpc2019/labwork/build$ ./labwork 1 ../data/eiffel.jpg 
USTH ICT Master 2018, Advanced Programming for HPC.
Warming up...
Starting labwork 1
labwork 1 CPU ellapsed 2766.4ms
labwork 1 ellapsed 661.9ms
\end{verbatim}

Obviously, the CUDA solution is much faster than the OpenMP solution (94.3ms compared to 661.9ms).
